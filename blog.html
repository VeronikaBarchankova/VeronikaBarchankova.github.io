About me

Welcome to my personal blog. I'm a 20 year old computer science student studying at the Faculty of Informatics and Information Technologies of the Slovak University of Technoogy in Bratislava.

Things that I'm actively learning about and bettering myself at include coding, knowledge and usage of Linux, Neovim, web development and making my own site. I used to do LeetCode actively, but currently put this activity on hiatus. Currently, I try to complete at least a few exercises from freeCodeCamp courses daily.

Some of my personal, for fun, interests include video games, such as the Persona and Yakuza series, literature, mainly works from Japanese authors, and music, I have a collection of music that I'm fond of in very many genres but Vocaloid music is probably the most interesting out of them.
